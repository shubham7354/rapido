 Rapido, India's largest bike taxi app, has  
 introduced Rapido Box, a new on-demand  
 delivery service where customers can request  
 pick and drop of food, groceries, and  
 medicines on the app, from or to another  
 customer. The person-to-person (P2P) on-  
 demand delivery service will enable users to  
 exchange food, groceries, or medicines with  
 their family and friends who are not able to  
 step out of their homes for essentials or have  
 run out of certain essential items in times of  
 need. The Rapido app will be the platform of
